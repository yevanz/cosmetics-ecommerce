At Glowify we value your privacy. This policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect How We Use Data Sharing & Disclosure Security Your Rights Contact

1. Data We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our services. Types of data we collect include:

  • Account info: name, email, shipping address, phone (when you register or purchase).
  • Payment data: processed via third-party payment providers — Glowify does not store full card details on our servers.
  • Usage data: pages visited, items viewed, device/browser metadata, IP address and the pages you visit.
  • Cookies & tracking: session cookies, analytics cookies and advertising identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To process orders, payments and deliver products.
  • To personalize your experience and recommend products.
  • To send transactional emails (order confirmations, shipping updates).
  • To analyze site performance and prevent fraud.

3. Sharing & Disclosure

We may share data with:

  • Payment processors and shipping partners to fulfill orders.
  • Service providers (analytics, hosting, customer support).
  • When required by law or to protect rights and safety.

4. Security

We use industry-standard controls such as TLS for data in transit, limited access controls, monitoring, and periodic security reviews. While we strive to protect data, no system is completely secure — please contact us immediately if you suspect misuse.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data (subject to legal/operational limits).
  • Opt out of marketing communications.
  • Withdraw consent for non-essential cookies (via cookie settings below).

6. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to keep you logged in, remember preferences, and to analyze usage. You can manage cookies using the button below; blocking cookies may reduce site functionality.
